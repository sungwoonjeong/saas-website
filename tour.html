Product

Our product is a brainstorm mapper at heart. It would help map out and organize different kinds of mind maps and brainstorming structures. It would offer automatic topic and idea suggestions, offer advice on relating different topics and ideas; All using AI assistance. There would also be options to take quick notes on the go.

Target Audience

We want to target design professionals, including those learning. Help them to get into the flow of brainstorming and concepting. It helps them branch out but also refine ideas. It helps organize their thoughts, streamlines their thought process. It also helps people who need to track ideas on the go.
